About Me


In Spring 2022, I made the bold decision to forgo my admission to the integrated Master’s in Professional Accounting (iMPA) program and instead pursue an entirely different path: business analytics. It’s a choice I’ve never regretted! Since then, I’ve seen firsthand how analytics, data science, and optimization can be applied to everything from optimizing delivery routes to predicting critical health outcomes. I've had the privilege of deepening my knowledge in these areas and sharing what I’ve learned with others. Now, I’m eager to contribute to the ongoing AI revolution.

Currently, I’m pursuing a Master of Science in Business Analytics at the University of Texas at Austin, where I previously earned my Bachelor’s in Business Administration in May 2024. As I continue to explore the complex world of data science and ML/AI, I’m committed to developing my expertise and navigating this rapidly evolving field.
